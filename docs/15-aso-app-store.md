# ASO and App Store plan

## iOS app positioning

Do not position as a medical sleep tracker. Position as a private sleep calculator and routine planner.

## Candidate app names

| Name | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| REMinder | Memorable English pun | Less obvious in Spanish |
| Sueño Claro | Strong Spanish/LatAm identity | Less global |
| SleepCue | Clear, short | Generic |
| WakeWell | Friendly | Broad/wellness saturated |

Recommended App Store display if English-first:

> REMinder: Sleep Calculator

Recommended Spanish subtitle:

> Calculadora de ciclos y siestas

## Keyword fields

## App Store description structure

1. One-line promise.
2. Privacy differentiator.
3. Features.
4. Premium benefits.
5. Disclaimer.
6. Subscription terms.

## Screenshot set

1. “Wake up between cycles.” — main result screen.
2. “Sleep now? Pick the best alarm time.”
3. “Nap without guessing.”
4. “Private by design: no account, no microphone.”
5. “Premium: reminders, widgets, no ads.”

## Review risk checklist

- Include subscription terms and privacy policy.
- Restore purchases visible.
- Premium benefits clear.
- No misleading medical claims.
- No hidden required account.
