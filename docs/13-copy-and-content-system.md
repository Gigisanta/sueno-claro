# Copy and content system

## Voice

Clear, calm, practical. No hype, no diagnosis, no fear.

## Hero copy variants

### English

1. Wake up between cycles, not in the middle of one.
2. Calculate your best bedtime in seconds.
3. A private sleep calculator. No account. No microphone. No tracking.

### Spanish

1. Despertate entre ciclos, no en el peor momento.
2. Calculá a qué hora dormir en segundos.
3. Una calculadora de sueño privada. Sin cuenta, sin micrófono, sin vueltas.

## Result microcopy

- Best: “Best option: gives you a full night with complete cycles.”
- Good: “Good option: practical balance if you cannot sleep earlier.”
- Minimum: “Minimum option: not ideal, but timed to reduce grogginess.”
- Nap: “Short nap: designed to avoid deep sleep.”

## Disclaimer copy

English:

> This calculator is for educational wellness planning only. Sleep cycles vary by person and night. It is not medical advice.

Spanish:

> Esta calculadora es una guía educativa de bienestar. Los ciclos de sueño varían según la persona y la noche. No es consejo médico.
