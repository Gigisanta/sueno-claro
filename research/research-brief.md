# Research brief

## Research question

Can a simple sleep cycle calculator become a monetizable web/PWA + iOS subscription product through better UX, privacy positioning, SEO, ads, and optional RevenueCat premium?

## Current answer

Yes, as a lean asset, not as a guaranteed venture-scale app. The right strategy is web-first utility with optional native premium. Avoid full sleep tracking and medical claims.

## Evidence summary

- RevenueCat shows Health & Fitness has strong subscription economics, but the web calculator should stay free to preserve SEO/trust.
- Sleep problems are common enough to support demand.
- Existing calculators are useful but often dated or content-heavy.
- Full trackers are differentiated enough that Sueño Claro should not compete head-on.

## Open questions for future validation

1. Which keyword cluster produces the best early impressions: English generic or Spanish long-tail?
2. Does result-sharing drive organic backlinks or social traffic?
3. Do users want reminders/widgets enough to pay?
4. Which ad density preserves trust?
5. Should the app brand be Sueño Claro, REMinder, or another ASO-driven name?
